## 1. The 2026 Visual Design System
We will not build a "Table of JSON." We will build a **"Command Center of Truth."**

### Component A: The "Glass Vault" (Glassmorphism)
*   **Visuals:** Frosted glass panels with vibrant, soft gradients in the background.
*   **Purpose:** Categorize data sources (B2B, E-com, Social) into distinct, layered cards that feel physically separated.
*   **Library:** Tailwind CSS + Custom HSL variable system for "Apple-like" color harmony.

### Component B: Micro-Interactions (The "Pulse")
