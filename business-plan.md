### 3.3 Pricing Strategy

**Freemium Model:**
- Free tier: 1,000 records/month
- Goal: Convert 10% to paid within 3 months
- Hook: Easy to start, hard to leave

**Value-Based Pricing:**
- Price based on value delivered, not cost
- E.g., if we save 40 hours/month @ $50/hour = $2,000 value
- Our price: $497/month (25% of value = 4:1 ROI)

**Tiered Pricing:**
- Psychological pricing ($497, not $500)
- Clear upgrade path
- Enterprise custom pricing

**Annual Discounts:**
- 20% off for annual commitment
- Improves cash flow and retention
